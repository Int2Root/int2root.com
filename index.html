About Int2Root

India-first.
Solo-founded.
Mission-driven.

Int2Root LLP was founded by Siddhartha Mukherjee with a clear belief: India's next billion internet users deserve tools that are built for them — not ported from Silicon Valley with a price tag attached.

Every product in the Int2Root portfolio is designed with Indian users, Indian pricing, and Indian languages as the starting point — not an afterthought. From free form builders to writing platforms for Indian creators, we're building the digital infrastructure for Bharat.

We believe great software should be accessible to a student in Patna just as much as a startup in Bengaluru. That's the root of everything we do.
